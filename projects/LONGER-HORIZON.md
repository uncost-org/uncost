# Longer-term studies and demonstrations

These concepts preserve the long-term direction without overpromising. They are not registered public projects and do not receive PRJ numbers until there is a separate evidence-based decision.

- Autonomous Basic-Needs Village
- Sector Automation Prototypes
- Care and Health Automation Studies
- Education and Tutoring Access
- Circular Materials and Repair
- Safety and Disaster Response

They remain research, design, and partner-development tracks until sufficient funding, local operators, safety review, legal capacity, insurance, permissions, and operational expertise exist. None implies that Uncost provides healthcare, diagnosis, treatment, personal care, child-facing services, enforcement, surveillance, or other regulated services.

# Repository conduct notice

> **Review status:** This public repository conduct notice is operational for participation in this GitHub space. The broader organizational POL-003 remains a draft pending independent and human review.

We welcome rigorous disagreement about evidence, costs, methods, assumptions, and trade-offs. We do not tolerate harassment, hate, threats, stalking, doxxing, sexual harassment, impersonation, spam, scams, partisan campaigning, or misuse of personal data.

Discuss ideas rather than attacking people. Cite evidence where possible. Respect privacy, accessibility needs, and boundaries. Disclose relevant conflicts when recommending vendors, partners, tools, or sources.

Report sensitive conduct or security issues privately through GitHub's private reporting mechanisms rather than a public issue. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to protect contributors and the project.

The full draft organizational policy is [POL-003](policies/POL-003-code-of-conduct.md).

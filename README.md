# Uncost

> **Living should not have a price tag.**
> **Uncost the cost of living.**

Uncost is a nonprofit, nonpartisan movement to significantly reduce the cost of living by putting AI and robotics to work for humanity.

The work starts with evidence: show what basic needs cost, identify where automation and shared infrastructure can plausibly reduce those costs, build open tools and playbooks, and track whether costs actually fall. When a proposed intervention does not work after full costs are counted, publish that result too.

## Current stage

Uncost is **pre-launch**. This repository is the fresh public records and open-work surface created from the final external Movement Plan and Roadmap. It does not mean the movement, website, policies, fundraising, Assembly, or projects are operational.

- The first focus sectors are **Shelter, Food, and Energy**.
- The flagship is the **Human Essentials Dashboard**.
- The first three projects form one system: PRJ-002 supplies data, PRJ-003 tests scenarios, and PRJ-001 presents them.
- Fiscal sponsorship is sought before any public tax-deductible donation launch.
- The policies and project one-pagers in this repository are **drafts pending independent Claude review and human review**. No policy is adopted or in force.
- No public cost figure should ship without a source, date, method, and confidence label.

See [REVIEW_STATUS.md](REVIEW_STATUS.md) for the exact review boundary.

## Hard boundaries

Uncost is not a political party or candidate campaign. It is not a crypto, DAO, token, or on-chain governance project. It does not promise that every physical constraint disappears or that everything becomes free. Healthcare, care, child-facing education, and safety-sensitive work remain studies until the required consent, privacy, safeguarding, legal, insurance, partner, and human-oversight gates are met.

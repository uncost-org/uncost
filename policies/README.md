## Action-specific gates

- Pledge/email: POL-002 plus reviewed notice, consent, age, deletion, and vendor controls.
- Donations: confirmed sponsor/channel plus POL-004/005/010 and professional review.
- Community/Assembly: POL-003/006, two moderators, privacy/safeguarding, and advisory-only launch.
- Public AI/agents: POL-007 plus human publication/privacy controls.
- Sensitive or physical activity: POL-008 plus funding, partner, permit/site, legal, safety, and insurance gates.
- Contribution intake/open-licence claims: POL-003/009 and verified applied licences.
- Lobbying/ballot activity: POL-001 plus written founder/sponsor/legal approval.

Publication does not satisfy any gate or authorize implementation.

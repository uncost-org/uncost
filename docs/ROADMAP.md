# Roadmap — movable targets anchored to Day 1

**Day 1 anchor:** 2026-07-15
**Hard deadlines:** no
**Rule:** targets move as evidence and capacity change; gates and stage order do not disappear because a date arrives.

| Source period | Movable target window | Main deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| First 90 days | 2026-07-15 through 2026-10-13 | Public-movement launch preparation; fiscal-sponsor application/due diligence; Pledge/email preparation; first source library; core policies; first public cost visual. Donation launch only after sponsorship confirmation. |

| Months 10–12 | approximately 2027-04-15 through 2027-07-15 | Public progress report; partner outreach; funding applications; Year 2 plan. |
| Year 2 | from approximately 2027-07-15 | Expand regions and sectors; develop PRJ-004 and PRJ-005 direction; grow contributors and leads; pursue grants/partners; assess whether recognition-method design is justified. |
| Year 3 | from approximately 2028-07-15 | Partner-led low-risk physical prototypes only where funding, legal, safety, technical, insurance, and operational gates are met; recognition pilot only after independent methodology exists. |
| Beyond | no fixed date | Larger integrated demonstrations only after evidence, governance, partners, and safety capacity mature. |

## Honest target ranges

The source plan describes subscriber, contributor, member, and partner ranges as floors to beat—not promises. They should not be promoted as commitments or used to bypass quality gates.

## Standing gates

- No tax-deductible donation launch before sponsor confirmation.
- No public cost claim without receipts and correction machinery.
- No formal Assembly vote before POL-006 and required legal/sponsor review.
- No vulnerable-person, healthcare, care, child-facing, safety-sensitive, or physical-pilot work before the relevant privacy, safeguarding, legal, insurance, partner, and human-oversight gates.
- No autonomous public posting or credential delegation by default.

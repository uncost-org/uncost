## Volunteer roles

> DOSSIER-BACKED: no dossier for this page; role list derives loosely from July source — The Case, The Projects, and Current ask (source library, methodology, sector dossiers, open tools).

Where volunteers help first

Useful roles, right now.

- **Source & license review** — Check that every figure has a real, licensed, dated source before it publishes.
- **Data methodology** — Help design how costs are measured, compared, and refreshed by region.
- **Sector research** — Write and maintain a sector dossier: scope, opportunity, guardrails.
- **Engineering** — Build the open tools — dashboards, trackers, and the data pipelines behind them.
- **Content & design** — Turn sourced evidence into short, honest, shareable explainers.
- **Translation & moderation** — Widen who can take part, and keep the community constructive.

Some volunteer roles may later become paid roles if funding allows — volunteering doesn’t guarantee employment or retroactive pay, and we’ll always say so plainly.

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source): the six discrete role definitions and the paid-conversion disclaimer are not in the July source; they are consistent with its evidence/tools/participation framing but authored here.

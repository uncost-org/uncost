## 05 How it works

> DOSSIER-BACKED: MOVEMENT_PLAN.md "How a cost gets uncosted" (six steps)

How a cost gets uncosted

Six steps, every time — negative results included.

1 Measure
the current cost and context, from public data.

2 Publish
sources, dates, methods, and confidence.

3 Break down
the cost stack into its real components.

4 Match
plausible AI, robotics, sharing, or infrastructure mechanisms to specific components.
⚠ DIVERGES FROM JULY SOURCE: export step 4 lists "AI, robotics, sharing, or infrastructure mechanisms" and omits "public-interest mechanisms," which the July source includes ("Match plausible AI, robotics, sharing, infrastructure, or public-interest mechanisms to specific components") — source wins.

5 Package
the result as open evidence, software, a brief, or a playbook local groups can use.

6 Track
whether total cost actually falls — publishing negative results too.

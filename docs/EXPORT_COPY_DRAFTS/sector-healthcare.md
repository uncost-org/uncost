## Worked example

> DOSSIER-BACKED: derives from the guardrail/opportunity framing of SEC-005 and the "How a cost gets uncosted" 6-step method in MOVEMENT_PLAN.md, but the sector-specific 6-step wording is design-authored elaboration

03 · How healthcare gets studied

A high-safety study — not a service Uncost provides.

1. Study where routine cost sits
Research shows where routine, repetitive labor drives cost in prevention, monitoring, and lab work — without touching clinical decisions.
⚠ UNCITED: worked-example step asserts "routine, repetitive labor drives cost in prevention, monitoring, and lab work" as fact — no figure/source; this is the sector "worked example" archetype (illustrative, unsourced).

2. Publish the sources
Sources and assumptions are posted openly so anyone can check them.
⚠ UNCITED: worked-example step (illustrative, unsourced archetype).

3. Break down with clinicians
The cost is examined with qualified professionals; nothing proceeds without their oversight.
⚠ UNCITED: worked-example step (illustrative, unsourced archetype).

4. Assess high-safety mechanisms
Where AI triage support, remote monitoring, or lab automation might reduce routine cost — always assistive, always human-supervised.
⚠ UNCITED: worked-example step (illustrative, unsourced archetype).

5. Package as research only
Uncost publishes findings as a study. It does not diagnose, treat, or deliver care, and any pilot needs consent, privacy, safeguarding, and legal review first.
⚠ UNCITED: worked-example step (illustrative, unsourced archetype).

6. Track with safeguards
Any real-world work is gated on regulation, insurance, and human oversight — and negative or unsafe findings are published too.
⚠ UNCITED: worked-example step (illustrative, unsourced archetype).

> DIVERGES FROM JULY SOURCE: MOVEMENT_PLAN.md "How a cost gets uncosted" names the canonical 6 steps as Measure / Publish / Break down / Match / Package / Track. The export relabels step 1 "Study" (vs "Measure") and step 4 "Assess high-safety mechanisms" (vs "Match"). Sector-specific framing, but the verb set diverges from the source method — source wins.

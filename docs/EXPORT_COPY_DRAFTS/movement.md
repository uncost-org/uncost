The Movement is how Uncost turns a widely felt frustration into organized momentum: reach people with something true and shareable, welcome them in, and offer a clear path to act. Every piece of content points back here — a place to join, learn more, or take part.

## How it reaches people

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

How it reaches people

Short, visual, source-led — never outrage alone.

Explainers, data pieces, and the movement's own illustrated style, distributed where attention already is — and carried further by values-aligned creators sharing with their own audiences. Public content must lead back to evidence and practical action.

## The path

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

The path from agreement to action

Support. Contribute. Partner.

**Support**
Free actions for anyone who agrees living costs too much: join, share sourced material, take the quiz, sign the Pledge. This builds the public demand that makes the rest possible.

**Contribute**
Back the work directly — volunteer research, data, engineering, writing, design, translation, or moderation; donate once the sponsor gate opens.

**Partner**
Experts, builders, institutions, and companies: build the tools, advise, coordinate a sector or region, or provide reviewed in-kind support.

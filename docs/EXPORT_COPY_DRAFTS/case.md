## Methodology drawer

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

Methodology — how The Receipts work

The rules

Every number has a source. Every source has a date. Every model states its assumptions. Where a claim cannot be sourced, it is labeled an estimate, scenario, or hypothesis — never presented as fact.

- Sources carry license, reuse status, and region coverage.
- Figures show visible "last updated" dates and a refresh cadence.
- Every correction is logged publicly.
- Negative results are published with the same prominence as wins.

_(Consistent with MOVEMENT_PLAN.md "How a cost gets uncosted" step 6 — publishing negative results too — and the receipts-first trust posture. No divergence.)_

Confidence labels

Confirmed · Estimate · Scenario · Needs refresh

Derived figures — calculated from two or more sources — display every contributing source, never a single badge.

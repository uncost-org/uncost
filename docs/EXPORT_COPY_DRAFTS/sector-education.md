## Worked example

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source) — structure derives from MOVEMENT_PLAN.md "How a cost gets uncosted" (Measure / Publish / Break down / Match / Package / Track); the education-specific content of each step is the archetype uncited worked example.

03 · How education gets uncosted

The worked example: widening low-cost adult learning.

1. Measure the cost pressure — Regional data shows what people pay for skills, tutoring, and access to learning — and where cost blocks opportunity.
⚠ UNCITED: "Regional data shows what people pay for skills, tutoring, and access to learning" — no source, no figures; the dossier registers no quantitative Education claim.

2. Publish the sources — Sources and assumptions are posted openly so anyone can check them.

3. Break down the cost — The cost is split into materials, tutoring time, access, and certification — starting with adult and open learning.
⚠ UNCITED: cost-stack components "materials, tutoring time, access, and certification" asserted with no source.

4. Match mechanisms to components — Open educational resources and low-cost AI learning support for adults — while child-facing tutoring stays a cautious future study with strict safeguards.

5. Package the playbook — Uncost publishes open resources and a playbook that libraries, community colleges, and nonprofits can run.
⚠ UNCITED: specific operator set "libraries, community colleges, and nonprofits" asserted with no source.

6. Track the result — The dashboard follows whether access widens and cost falls. Child safety, privacy, curriculum quality, and human oversight gate any child-facing work.

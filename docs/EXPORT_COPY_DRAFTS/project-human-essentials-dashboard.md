## Overview

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

The flagship of The Case.

> DOSSIER-BACKED: Snapshot — Role in the system ("presentation layer of a three-part system... PRJ-002 supplies... PRJ-003 supplies...") + The problem and the cost being reduced

The Human Essentials Dashboard presents what people need, what those needs cost by region, how costs compare to income, how they change over time, and which reduction paths look most promising. It is the presentation layer over two underlying systems — the Cost-of-Living Tracker supplies the data, and the Basic Needs Cost Model tests scenarios.

> DOSSIER-BACKED: MVP deliverables #1 (honest coverage banner "Data available for N of M regions" — N is whatever is real, even if it is 2)

Regionality is built in from the start: a single global cost number would damage credibility, because rent, food, energy, wages, climate, and public services vary drastically by place. Early versions cover only a few regions and label coverage plainly — for example, "data available for three regions" — and expand from there.

⚠ UNCITED: "data available for three regions" — the dossier's coverage-banner example is "Data available for N of M regions ... even if it is 2"; the export fixes the illustrative number at "three." Illustrative per the export's own "for example" framing, but the specific number is not sourced.
⚠ DIVERGES FROM JULY SOURCE: dossier MVP scope is "2–3 launch regions"; export's example says "three" — minor, source wins on the 2–3 range.

> DOSSIER-BACKED: What it is / what it is not + AI role and human gates ("No figure publishes without a human-verified SRC- entry") + Overview mapping claim

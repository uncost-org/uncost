## Intro — body

The Projects are how Uncost moves from evidence to action: open-source tools, data models, and automation playbooks that communities, co-ops, nonprofits, public agencies, and builders can run themselves. We don't own the farm — we publish the model, the sources, and the playbook so others can adopt and improve them.

## Later

Later — studies & demonstrations

Gated on funding and safety.

None involves Uncost directly providing healthcare, care, child-facing education, or safety services.

## How they help

The honest chain

Every project has to earn its cost claim.

A project only counts as a cost-reduction path where energy, capital, maintenance, and local conditions make a net reduction plausible. Where the model shows it doesn't beat the status quo, we publish that negative result too. Where a cost driver is policy rather than technology, our role is open data and nonpartisan analysis — never partisan lobbying.

## 05 How it works

> DOSSIER-BACKED: MOVEMENT_PLAN.md → How a cost gets uncosted (six steps)

How a cost gets uncosted

Six steps, every time — negative results included.

1 — Measure the current cost and context, from public data.
⚠ DIVERGES FROM JULY SOURCE: export adds "from public data"; MOVEMENT_PLAN.md step 1 reads "Measure the current cost and context." (no data-source qualifier) — source wins.

2 — Publish sources, dates, methods, and confidence.

3 — Break down the cost stack into its real components.

4 — Match plausible AI, robotics, sharing, or infrastructure mechanisms to specific components.
⚠ DIVERGES FROM JULY SOURCE: export omits "public-interest"; MOVEMENT_PLAN.md step 4 reads "Match plausible AI, robotics, sharing, infrastructure, or public-interest mechanisms" — source wins.

5 — Package the result as open evidence, software, a brief, or a playbook local groups can use.

6 — Track whether total cost actually falls — publishing negative results too.

## Introband

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

The rules we hold ourselves to.

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

Because Uncost handles donations, collects email and community input, builds public data tools, and explores sensitive sectors, it commits to a clear set of policies from the start. ## How policies change

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

How a policy changes

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source)

Version-controlled, in the open.

> DOSSIER-BACKED: sensitive-sector clause derives from July source — CONTROL.md date/gating rules and CLAUDE.md boundaries ("Healthcare, care, child-facing education ... remain gated"; "Stage order, evidence, legal review, safety, sponsor requirements ... outrank dates")

Every policy lives in the public record with its full history. Change one, and the reference number stays while the version increments — so you can always see what it said before, when it changed, and why. Sensitive sectors — healthcare, care, and child-facing education — are held to the highest bar and stay studies until consent, privacy, safeguarding, legal review, and human oversight are firmly in place.

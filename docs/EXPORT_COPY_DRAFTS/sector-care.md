## Worked example

> DESIGN-AUTHORED (no source): SEC-006 contains no worked example; these six Care-specific steps are authored for the page. They adapt the July source's generic "How a cost gets uncosted" sequence into a care framing.

03 · How care gets studied

A high-safety study — support for carers, not replacement.

1. Study where carers are stretched
Research shows where scheduling, logistics, and routine tasks consume carers' time — without touching the human relationship at the heart of care.

2. Publish the sources
Sources and assumptions are posted openly so anyone can check them.

3. Break down with carers and families
The work is examined with carers, families, and disability advocates, whose consent and dignity govern everything.

4. Assess assistive mechanisms
Where assistive robotics, scheduling tools, or caregiver support might reduce burden and cost — always supporting people, never replacing them.

5. Package as research only
Uncost publishes findings as a study. It does not provide care, and any pilot needs consent, safeguarding, and legal review first.

6. Track with safeguards
Real-world work is gated on safeguarding and human oversight; concerns and negative findings are published too.
